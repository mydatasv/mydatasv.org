September 13: Monthly Full-Moon Mixer

New solutions by new companies: Personal data startups!

Investors love it. Consumers hate it. Surveillance capitalism is everywhere, but is it all we can do?

Join us in our second mixer focused on ethics, privacy and personal data, for thought-provoking presentations, wine and chips, and networking with fellow travelers intrigued by the possibilities and pitfalls of personal data.

Agenda:

6:00pm Doors open and networking
6:30pm Introduce yourself — everybody gets to speak up, briefly
6:45pm Lightening talk presentations by a selection of startup companies that think they can do better than surveillance capitalism, and focus on the opportunities of personal data without disrespecting customer privacy and agency. Find out what's coming!

We are currently reaching out to startups to present. If you are interested in presenting, contact us on Slack.

Where: At Node, 1011 Kearny Street, San Francisco, CA 94133
